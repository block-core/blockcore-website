Chain Setup

We at Blockcore believe in making blockchain technology easier and more approachable for every developer, so part of this guides us in how we build our technologies. We attempt to make our technology easily customizeable through setup and configuration, as oppose to code changes.

We host a list of setup and configurations for the platform tooling on Blockcore.

Files available here are for more than Blockcore based blockchains, it is configuration for other chains that our tools support. For example it is possible to use the Blockcore Indexer and our Blockcore Explorer to index and view Bitcoin blocks and transaction history.

The Blockcore team is not responsible for the accuracy and correctness of setup files on this site. Individual chain owners is responsible for keeping their setup up-to-date.

You can add your own awesome blockchain by forking our GitHub repo and provide a pull request for review and approval. Projects that are unresponsive and incompatible with our tooling, risk being removed.

We reserve the right to remove any of the configurations, without warning.

https://github.com/block-core/chaininfo
