Wallet

Blockcore Wallet is a Blockcore, Stratis and Cirrus Non-Custodial HD wallet in your browser for Coins, Tokens, Identities (DID), NFTs and more.

Multi Coin Wallet
You can make multiple wallet with accounts that span multiple different blockchains, including Bitcoin.
Tokens and NFTs
You can view your tokens and NFTs for blockchains that support those features.
Purchase and Swap
Within the wallet, you can swap coins between certain blockchains, and you can purchase and exchange using third party services.
